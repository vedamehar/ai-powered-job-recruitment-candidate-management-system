# Deployment Monitoring & Post-Deployment Support

To encourage adoption, the system was designed to seamlessly integrate AI insights into daily recruitment workflows.

## Adoption Strategies
- Embedded AI and Agentforce components directly on:
  - Home Page
  - Candidate Application Record Page
  - Lightning App
- Minimal learning curve using **standard Salesforce UI**
- Clear labels and descriptions for AI-generated insights
- Automated AI recommendations to reduce manual hiring decisions

## Monitoring & Post-Deployment Support
After deployment, continuous monitoring ensured system reliability and user satisfaction.

### Monitoring Activities
- Tracked usage of AI candidate scoring and recommendations
- Monitored hiring conversion rates
- Reviewed approval turnaround times
- Checked performance of flows and scheduled automations

### Support Activities
- Addressed recruiter and manager feedback
- Fine-tuned AI scoring thresholds based on real hiring data
- Improved UI labels and help text
- Enhanced AI recommendation clarity and relevance

## Business Impact & Outcomes
The successful deployment of the AI-Powered Recruitment System resulted in:

- Faster hiring decisions
- Better prioritization of high-quality candidates
- Reduced manual evaluation effort
- Improved offer approval efficiency
- Data-driven hiring decisions using AI insights
- Increased productivity of recruitment teams
